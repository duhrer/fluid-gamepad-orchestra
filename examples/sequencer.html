Fluid Gamepad - "Sequencer"

Each gamepad controls its own sequence of pulses (vibrations).

Controls

Press any button on your controller to make it show up here (it's a requirement of the Gamepad API). Once your controller shows up, you can:

  1. Use the d-pad to navigate the grid (sequence).
  2. Press Button 0 (X on a Playstation controller) to set the strong and weak vibration value for the focused step to 100%.
  3. Button 1 (circle on a Playstation controller) sets the strong and weak vibration value for the focused step to 0.
  4. Button 2 (square on a Playstation controller) sets the strong vibration for the focused step to 100% and the weak to 0%.
  5. Button 3 (triangle on a Playstation controller) sets the strong vibration for the focused step to 0% and the weak to 100%.
  6. Press down on either of the thumb sticks to stop/start the sequencer.
  7. Use the bumpers to control the BPM.
